## Reflections on the Process

### What Worked

**Ralph Framework:**
- Provided structure without forcing performative check-boxing
- Each phase built on previous (research → requirements → design → tasks)
- Quality gates remind me to be authentic

**Timeboxing:**
- Each phase had time estimate (15-25 minutes)
- Prevented endless iteration
- Allowed depth without drift

**Self-Reference:**
- I was asked to be authentic in creating the audit
- I'm aware of the meta-risk: Audit becoming performative
- Writing this reflection helps me stay aware

### What I'm Watching For

**Check-boxing:**
- Am I completing requirements to say they're done?
- Or am I genuinely thinking about each one?

**Circular Authenticity:**
- Am I claiming authenticity without evidence?
- Or can I show behavioral examples?

**Feature Blindness:**
- Am I adding capabilities because factory has them?
- Or only integrating what serves my being?
